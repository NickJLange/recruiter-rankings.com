    What we’re building

    Public directory

    Browse aggregated, de‑identified metrics — no PII in public. Suppression thresholds ensure k‑anonymity.

    Right‑of‑reply

    Verified recruiters can respond to reviews. Takedown policy defaults to safety while we learn.

    Future: Benchmarks

    Time‑to‑first‑response, response rate, candidate satisfaction, peer comparisons — opt‑in identity only.

    Quality dimensions (1–5)

    • Responsiveness
    • Clarity/accuracy of role description
    • Understanding candidate needs
    • Fairness and inclusivity
    • Timeline management
    • Feedback quality
    • Professionalism/respect
    • Job–role match quality

    Verification (POC)

    LinkedIn profile challenge: we generate a one‑time token that you place on your profile; we verify and never store plaintext tokens.

    Moderation

    PII scrubbers → low‑cost LLM heuristics → human review for flagged cases. Takedown SLA: 7 days standard, 3 days urgent.
